Research Projects

Sketchy PDE-Constrained Optimization

This is my current research in collaboration with Prof. Madeleine Udell and Dr. Drew Kouri.

PDE-Constrained Optimization is often bottlenecked by the storage requirements and our work aims to rectify this by developing novel low memory optimzation algorithms.

Software Projects

Presolve Routines for Optimization Problems

This was a Google Summer of Code 2016 project under Julia-Opt under the supervision of Prof. Madeleine Udell.

Currently the package contains an implementation of LP Presolving based on ideas from Andersen & Andersen paper and the GLPK solver. Presolving removes redundancies from the original problem given by the user and constructs a smaller equivalent problem which is then fed to the solver
